3.1.2

Model Putaran Stesen
Bahasa Inggeris SJK
Tahun 2

Title: The Old House

Learning Standard

  • Understand the main idea of simple sentences.
  • Understand specific information and details of simple sentences.

Learning Objectives

At the end of the lesson pupils can:

  • State 4 main ideas of simple sentences.
  • State 4 specific information and details of simple sentences.

Assessment

  • Worksheet.
  • Kahoot.

Computer and Internet

Station 1

  • Pupils listen to the song “The Old House”.
  • Pupils read the lyric of the song.

Station 2

  • Teacher explains about the song.
  • Teacher gives task to the pupils by group.

Station 3

  • Pupils work and discuss in group.
  • Pupils complete worksheets.
  • Quiz Kahoot.
